# CinemAt Generator

**Every aspect ratio mat you need, in one place.**

Load a frame grab from your edit, pick a format, preview it instantly, and export a frame-accurate overlay — ready to drop straight into Premiere, Resolve, or Avid.

[Download on the Mac App Store](#){: .button}

---

## The Problem

There's no single place in your NLE to pull a standard mat and see it on your footage. You end up Googling pixel dimensions, digging through old projects, or building it by hand every single time.

CinemAt Generator fixes that.

---

## How It Works

1. **Load a frame grab** from your edit — JPG, PNG, or TIF
2. **Pick an aspect ratio** from 17 industry-standard formats, or enter any custom ratio
3. **Preview instantly** — the mat updates in real time
4. **Export** the mat overlay or a full composite at your source image's native resolution
