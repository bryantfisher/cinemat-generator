# CineMat Generator

**Every aspect ratio mat you need, in one place.**

Load a frame grab from your edit, pick a format, preview it instantly, and export a frame-accurate overlay for Premiere, Resolve, or Avid.

---

## The Problem

Every editor knows it. You need a 2.39 letterbox mat for a Scope delivery, or your client wants to see how the cut feels in 1.85 — and there's no single place in your NLE to just pull a standard mat and see it on your footage. You end up Googling pixel dimensions, digging through old projects, or building it by hand every time.

CinemAt Generator fixes that.

---

## What It Does

- **Load any frame grab** — JPG, PNG, or TIF from your edit
- **Choose from 17 industry-standard aspect ratios** — from vertical 9:16 all the way to 2.76 widescreen
- **Enter any custom ratio** — type in whatever your delivery spec requires
- **Preview in real time** — the mat updates instantly as you switch formats
- **Export two ways:**
  - **Mat only** — a PNG with a transparent active window, ready to drop into your timeline as an overlay
  - **Composite** — your frame with the mat burned in, for client references and approvals
